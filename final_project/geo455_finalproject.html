Mapping Skyscrapers and Wealth

About this assignment

This project is based on over a dozen of the tallest buildings in the world. You can find more information about these amazing architechtural achievements at the Skyscraper Center Website. In addition to skyscraper information, this project includes a selection of the richest cities in the world, many of which are home to these skyscrapers as these buildings cost millions to construct.
